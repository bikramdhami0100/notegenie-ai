# NoteGenie AI

An intelligent study companion that generates structured syllabi and detailed notes with real-life examples and video references using Gemini 2.5 Flash.

## Features

* **Generate Syllabus**: Enter a subject and NoteGenie AI will generate a comprehensive syllabus with topics structured from Basic to Advanced levels.
* **Generate Notes**: Select a topic and NoteGenie AI will generate detailed notes with real-life examples, analogies, and video references.
* **Search YouTube**: NoteGenie AI will suggest relevant YouTube search queries for each topic, allowing you to find video tutorials and explanations.
* **Save and Load**: Save your syllabus and notes to local storage or cloud storage, and load them later.
* **Responsive Design**: NoteGenie AI is designed to work on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

## How it Works

1. **Gemini 2.5 Flash**: NoteGenie AI uses the Gemini 2.5 Flash model to generate high-quality content based on human instructions.
2. **Structured Data**: NoteGenie AI generates structured data in the form of a syllabus and notes, which can be easily parsed and used by other applications.
3. **YouTube Search Queries**: NoteGenie AI suggests relevant YouTube search queries for each topic, allowing you to find video tutorials and explanations.
